What We Do

We are in the buisness of procuring organic, fertilizer and pesticide free edible products from the farmers and small scale self-help group organizations, who process it and repack it for retail consumption, and deliver it to the customers directly.

Our Purpose

This process helps both the consumers and the farmers, thereby ensuring quality products delivered directly to consumers at a reasonable price.
